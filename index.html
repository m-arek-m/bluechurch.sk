Official opening hours

St. Elisabeth Roman Catholic church in Bratislava (also called "The Blue Church") is open daily for services and prayers. There is no Entrance Fee. We kindly ask you to maintain silence in the church.

Extraordinary opening of the Church

An extraordinary opening of the church is possible for group of pilgrims that wants to celebrate Catholic Holy mass in our Church. There must be a Catholic priest within this group. This situation must be agreed in advance trough email.
